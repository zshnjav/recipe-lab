## Prep (Mise-en-Place)

1. Dice yellow onion, carrots, and celery. Mince garlic.
2. Drain and rinse Great Northern beans. Measure pasta, broth, and seasonings.

## Execution

1. Heat pot over medium-high. Add olive oil and brown ground beef 5–7 minutes. Drain excess fat if needed.
2. Add onion, carrots, celery. Cook 6–8 minutes until softened. Add garlic and cook 30 seconds.
3. Stir in Italian seasoning, salt, and pepper. Add diced tomatoes and tomato sauce. Simmer 2–3 minutes.
4. Add beans and chicken broth. Bring to boil, reduce to simmer, cook uncovered 15 minutes.
5. Stir in ditalini pasta. Simmer 8–10 minutes until tender. Add splash of water/broth if too thick.
6. Taste and adjust seasoning. Turn off heat. Top with fresh basil and parmesan if desired.
